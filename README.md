# WastelessHarvest-frondend 


This project aims to address the critical issue of food waste by creating a comprehensive food waste management system. With Wasteless Harvest, food suppliers can easily list their excess food inventory, specifying details such as quantity, expiry date, location, and contact information. This allows them to contribute to reducing waste and making a positive social impact. Recipients, including orphanages, old-age homes, and charitable organizations, can access Wasteless Harvest to discover available surplus food, view detailed information about each listing, and directly connect with suppliers for pick-up or delivery. By efficiently utilizing excess food, the project helps meet the nutritional needs of those in need.
